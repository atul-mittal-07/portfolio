About Me

Hi there! My name is Atul Mittal and I am a frontend developer with 3+ years of relevant experience in the frontend development and total of 6+ years in Software development field. In addition to my technical skills, I am a strong problem-solver and thrive in fast-paced, collaborative environments. I am constantly looking for new ways to improve my skills and stay up-to-date with the latest technologies and best practices in the field.
